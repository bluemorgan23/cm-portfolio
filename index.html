About Me

I first recognized my passion for software development in the middle of an Object-Oriented Programming course I took during high school. At the time, I knew I wanted to learn more about this passion, but I didn't quite jump right into the field. I moved to Colorado from Tennessee to find out more about the Western United States. I explored different industries such as retail management and restaurant service, but never felt fulfillment from these rolls.


After this realization, I decided to take my life in a different direction by moving to Nashville where I enrolled in Nashville Software School. I have to admit that this decision was one of the better choices I've ever made for myself. The fulfillment of creatively solving problems with new and exciting technologies has reassured me that my passion for software development is very real. All I needed to do was find the right kind of challenge, and the rest is history!
